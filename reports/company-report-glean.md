## What They Do

Glean provides an AI-powered enterprise search and work assistant platform that connects to 100+ enterprise SaaS applications (Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Slack, Salesforce, Jira, etc.) to help employees find information scattered across systems. Using natural language processing and a personalized knowledge graph, Glean improves search relevance and enables content generation. In 2025, they expanded into **AI Agents** — autonomous workflows that can reason, adapt, and complete tasks using enterprise context.
