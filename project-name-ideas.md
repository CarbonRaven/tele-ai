# AI Payphone Project - Name & Domain Ideas

## Project Concept
A self-contained payphone running a local AI. Users interact with the AI to get information, jokes, etc. using a 90s-style payphone. Functions fashioned after 1990s telephone services and information as well as more modern functions.

## Top Picks Summary

### Most Evocative
- **At The Tone** - Instantly recognizable, mysterious
- **Coin Return** - Physical, satisfying, nostalgic

### Most Obscure/Cool
- **KLondike-5** - Deep cut, cinematic feel
- **2600** - Hacker cred, insider knowledge
- **Pay Station** - Proper terminology, clean

### Most Marketable
- **Time & Temp** - Universally understood service
- **The Time Lady** - Character-driven, story potential
- **QuarterMind** - Immediately evokes the payphone experience
- **Dial Tone** - Universal recognition, clean

### Most Unique
- **The Singing Wire** - Poetic, unusual
- **Fortress Phone** - Actual product name, tough vibe
- **Blue Box** - Legendary, edgy

### Best Nostalgia
- **Dial-A-Mind** - Perfect 90s service name format
- **The Last Payphone** - Romantic, story-driven

### Best Marketing Potential
- **Please Hold** - Great tagline possibilities
- **Off The Hook** - Works as slang and literal
- **Collect Call** - "You have a collect call from... the future"
