
### Hardware
- Raspberry Pi AI HAT+ 2 (Hailo-10H)
- Raspberry Pi 5/16GB (pi-voice, 10.10.10.10)
- Raspberry Pi 5/16GB (pi-ollama, 10.10.10.11)
- 5-port gigabit switch
- Grandstream HT801 v2 (ATA, 10.10.10.12)
- Payphone (analog)

### Power Supply
- Lightning Ofertas 500W GaN IV 10-Port Charging Station (Amazon B0FL7PWF49)
  - 7x USB-C (PD 3.0/PPS, 140W max per port)
  - 3x USB-A
  - 500W total capacity
  - Input: 100-240V AC, 50/60Hz

### Power Budget

| Device                      | Connector | Max Draw | Typical Draw |
|-----------------------------|-----------|----------|--------------|
| Pi #1 (pi-voice) + AI HAT+ | USB-C     | 25W      | 15-20W       |
| Pi #2 (pi-ollama)           | USB-C     | 25W      | 10-15W       |
| Grandstream HT801 v2        | USB-A     | 5W       | 3W           |
| 5-port GbE switch           | USB-A     | 5W       | 3-4W         |
| **Total**                   |           | **60W**  | **31-42W**   |

500W supply at ~8-12% utilization. Ample headroom.

### Notes
- Pi 5 requires USB-C PD cable rated for 5A to avoid under-voltage warnings
- HT801 and switch need USB-A to barrel/micro-USB adapter cables
