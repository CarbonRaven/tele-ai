## Inside Cover - Instructions

### HOW TO USE YOUR AI PAYPHONE

**Making a Call**
1. Lift the handset
2. Wait for dial tone
3. Dial the 7-digit service number (see directory listings)
4. Follow voice prompts

**Navigation**
- Press **\*** (star) at any time to return to the main menu
- Dial **555-0000** to reach the Operator
- Press **9** to repeat the last message
- Say "menu" to return to the main menu

**Voice Commands**
You may speak naturally to the Operator, or say the name of any service:
- "Jokes" - connects to Dial-A-Joke
- "Weather" - connects to Weather Forecast
- "Time" - connects to Time & Temperature

**Tips**
- Speak clearly into the handset
- Wait for the beep before speaking
- The AI Operator can help you find any service
