# AI Payphone Features List

Based on research of iconic 1980s-1990s telephone services, reimagined for a local AI-powered payphone.

---

## Core Navigation

### Main Menu System
- Touch-tone navigation (press 1 for X, press 2 for Y)
- Voice command option ("Say 'jokes' or press 1")
- "Operator" mode - free-form conversation with AI
- Return to main menu with * or "menu"
- Hang up detection and graceful goodbye

### The Operator (Default AI Mode)
- Free-form conversation with the AI
- Dial 0 at any time to reach "the operator"
- Personality: helpful, slightly formal, retro telephone operator demeanor
- Can transfer to any other service ("Let me connect you to our joke line...")

---

## Information Services

### Time & Temperature (Dial: 411 or say "Time")
- Current time with vintage delivery: "At the tone, the time will be 3:45 PM and 20 seconds... *BEEP*"
- Current temperature and conditions
- Fake vintage sponsor: "Brought to you by First National Bank of Somewhere"
- Optional: World clock for other cities

### Weather Forecast (Dial: 936 / WEA)
- Current conditions
- Today's forecast
- Extended forecast
- Vintage delivery style with sponsor mentions
- Optional: Marine forecast, ski conditions (as jokes or real data)

### Daily Horoscope (Dial: 467 / HOR)
- Ask for zodiac sign
- AI-generated daily horoscope in vintage style
- Mystical background music/tones
- "For your lucky numbers, press 1..."

### News Headlines (Dial: 639 / NEW)
- Top news stories summarized
- Categories: World, local, sports, entertainment
- Vintage newsreader delivery style

### Sports Scores (Dial: 776 / SPO)
- Current scores and standings
- Yesterday's results
- Upcoming games
- Delivered like a 1-900 sports line

## Entertainment Services

### Dial-A-Joke (Dial: 565 / JOK)
- AI-generated jokes
- Categories:
  - Clean/family jokes
  - Puns and wordplay
  - Dad jokes
  - Topical humor
  - Classic one-liners
- "For another joke, press 1. To hear that joke again, press 2."

### Trivia Challenge (Dial: 874 / TRI)
- Multiple choice trivia questions
- Categories: History, science, pop culture, sports, geography
- Keep score across multiple questions
- Different difficulty levels
- "You got 7 out of 10! You're a trivia champion!"

### Story Time (Dial: 786 / STO)
- Short AI-generated stories (1-3 minutes)
- Genres: Mystery, sci-fi, fairy tales, horror, comedy
- Serialized stories ("Press 1 for the next chapter")
- Bedtime stories mode (calming delivery)

### Would You Rather (Dial: 968 / WYR)
- Classic would-you-rather questions
- AI discusses your choice
- Tracks popular answers

### Fortune Teller (Dial: 368 / FOR)
- Mystical AI persona
- Ask a yes/no question, get a mystical answer
- Tarot-style readings
- Magic 8-ball mode
- Crystal ball predictions

### Mad Libs (Dial: 623 / MAD)
- Interactive word game
- AI asks for nouns, verbs, adjectives
- Reads back the silly story

## Special Features

- Different featured service each day
- "Today's special: Call the fortune teller for free!"
- Encourages repeat visits

### Secret Codes
- Hidden dial codes unlock easter eggs
- Konami code equivalent (Up Up Down Down...)
- Secret menus and hidden personas
- Rewards for exploration

### Achievements System
- Track caller milestones
- "You've called 10 times! You're a regular!"
- Unlock new features with usage
- Verbal "badges" announced

### Holiday Modes
- Special content for holidays
- Halloween: Spooky stories, horror jokes
- Christmas: Carols, holiday cheer
- Valentine's: Love advice, poetry
- April Fools: Everything is a prank

### Late Night Mode
- Different tone after midnight
- More philosophical conversations
- "Night owl" persona
- Calmer, more contemplative

## Technical Features

### Interaction Patterns
- Timeout handling ("Are you still there?")
- Repeat last message ("Press 9 to hear that again")
- Volume boost option
- Slower speech option for accessibility

### Session Management
- Remember caller within session
- "Welcome back!" for repeat interactions
- Continue where you left off (stories, trivia)

### Error Handling
- Graceful handling of unrecognized input
- Vintage error messages ("I'm sorry, I didn't understand. Please try again.")
- "Operator assistance" fallback
