## Search Strategies

### For YouTube:
- Use exact phone numbers in quotes: `"867-5309"`
- Add "recording" or "call": `"Ghostbusters hotline" recording`
- Include year for vintage content: `"1-900" commercial 1989`

### For Internet Archive:
- Search by voice talent: `Jane Barbe`, `Audichron`
- Search by service type: `"time and temperature"`, `"1-900"`
- Browse VHS collections for embedded commercials

### For Reddit:
- r/ObscureMedia - Rare/forgotten recordings
- r/nostalgia - 1-900 number discussions
- r/VintageAds - Phone commercials
- r/SquaredCircle - Wrestling hotline recordings
